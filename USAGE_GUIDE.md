## üí¨ Discord

### How to Add Custom Emojis

**Requirements:**
- Server owner or "Manage Emojis" permission
- PNG format with transparent background (recommended)
- Max file size: 256 KB
- Recommended dimensions: 128x128px

**Steps:**

1. **Download Your Emoji**
   - Generate your emoji on Deep Vortex
   - Click the "üì• Download" button
   - Save the PNG file to your computer

2. **Open Server Settings**
   - Right-click on your Discord server icon
   - Select "Server Settings"
   - Navigate to "Emoji" in the left sidebar

3. **Upload Your Emoji**
   - Click "Upload Emoji" button
   - Select your downloaded PNG file
   - Give it a short, memorable name (e.g., `astronautcat`)
   - Click "Save"

4. **Use Your Emoji**
   - In any channel, type `:` followed by your emoji name
   - Example: `:astronautcat:` will display your emoji
   - Or click the emoji picker and search for your custom emoji

**Discord Limits:**
- **Free Server**: 50 emoji slots
- **Level 1 Boost**: 100 emoji slots
- **Level 2 Boost**: 150 emoji slots
- **Level 3 Boost**: 250 emoji slots

**Pro Tips:**
- Use simple, descriptive names
- Test on both dark and light themes
- Animated GIFs require server boost

**Requirements:**

## üéÆ Twitch

### How to Add Channel Emotes

**Requirements:**
- Twitch Affiliate or Partner status
- PNG format with transparent background
- Three sizes required: 28x28px, 56x56px, 112x112px
- Max 1 MB per file

**Steps:**

1. **Prepare Your Emote**
   - Generate on Deep Vortex
   - Download the image
   - Resize to three versions: 28px, 56px, 112px
   - Use tools like [Photopea](https://photopea.com) for resizing

2. **Access Creator Dashboard**
   - Go to [twitch.tv/dashboard](https://twitch.tv/dashboard)
   - Navigate to "Settings" ‚Üí "Affiliate/Partner"
   - Click "Emotes" section

3. **Upload Emote**
   - Click "Upload Emotes"
   - Upload all three sizes (28px, 56px, 112px)
   - Name your emote (6-25 characters)
   - Select emote tier (subscriber tier)
   - Click "Submit"

4. **Wait for Approval**
   - Twitch reviews emotes (usually 24-48 hours)
   - Check email for approval notification
   - Once approved, subscribers can use it!

**Twitch Emote Slots:**
- **Affiliate**: 1 emote per tier (5 total)
- **Partner**: 5 emotes per tier + more as you grow

**Emote Guidelines:**
- No copyrighted content
- No offensive imagery
- Must be clearly visible at 28x28px
- Follow [Twitch Emote Guidelines](https://help.twitch.tv/s/article/emote-guidelines)

**Pro Tips:**
- Test visibility at smallest size (28x28px)
- Use high contrast for readability
- Simple designs work best
- Create variations for different tiers

## üì± WhatsApp

### How to Add Personal Stickers

**Requirements:**
- Third-party sticker app
- PNG or WebP format
- Size: 512x512px
- Max file size: 100 KB

**Steps:**

1. **Download Sticker App**
   - **iOS**: "Sticker Maker Studio" or "Sticker.ly"
   - **Android**: "Personal Stickers" or "Sticker.ly"

2. **Create Sticker Pack**
   - Open the app
   - Tap "Create New Pack"
   - Name your pack
   - Add your downloaded emojis (min 3 stickers)

3. **Add to WhatsApp**
   - Tap "Add to WhatsApp" in the app
   - Confirm
   - Open WhatsApp

4. **Use Your Stickers**
   - Open any chat
   - Tap sticker icon (square smiley face)
   - Your pack appears with other stickers
   - Tap to send!

**WhatsApp Limits:**
- Min 3 stickers per pack
- Max 30 stickers per pack
- Tray icon required (96x96px)
- Max 100 KB per sticker

**Pro Tips:**
- Compress with [Squoosh](https://squoosh.app) if over 100 KB
- Remove background for best results
- Create multiple themed packs
- Share pack links with friends

## ‚ùì Troubleshooting

### "File size too large"

**Solution:**
1. Use [Squoosh](https://squoosh.app)
2. Compress image quality to 80-90%
3. Or resize dimensions smaller

### "Background not transparent"

**Solution:**
1. Visit [remove.bg](https://remove.bg)
2. Upload your emoji
3. Download PNG with transparency
4. Re-upload to platform

### "Emoji looks blurry"

**Solution:**
- Generate a new one (AI variance)
- Use sharpen filter in Photopea
- Ensure you're not upscaling (only downsize)

### "Can't upload emoji"

**Check:**
- Do you have permission? (mod/admin/boost)
- Is file format PNG?
- Is file size within limits?
- Are dimensions correct?
